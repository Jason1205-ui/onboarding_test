How do I search for products?

+

You can use product names or product notification numbers to search for products. Simply enter your search term in the search box on our Search Products page and select the appropriate search type.

What is the product notification number?

+

The product notification number is a unique identifier for a cosmetic product, typically starting with "NOT". In Malaysia, the number is issued by the National Pharmaceutical Regulatory Agency (NPRA).

Can I filter products by safety level or harmful ingredients?

+

Yes. Use the filter options in the Search Products section to narrow results by:

  • Safety Level - Low, Medium, or High Risk
  • Approval Status - Compliant Pending, Violation, or Unknown
  • Ingredient Profile - Mercury-Free, Hydroquinone-Free, Lead-Free, etc.

Why is an ingredient marked as "restricted" rather than "banned"?

+

"Restricted" means the ingredient is allowed only in specific concentrations or applications under regulatory guidelines.

"Banned" means it's not permitted at all in cosmetics due to safety concerns.
